Windows Apps.

SoftwareMK provides Windows Phone apps and Windows application development services for Windows Phone and Windows 8.

Windows Phone Apps Our High quality Windows Phone apps

  • IRC7

    IRC7 is the long waited IRC app for Windows Phone 7. IRC7 uses the pieces provided by the Windows Phone 7 Mango-update to provide you the ultimate IRC client for Windows Phone 7.

  • Movie Posters

    Find great wallpapers with Movie Posters. Just enter the name of the movie and you will get a high resolution posters provided by the extensive TMDb. Simple and fast to use.

  • IP Snoop

    What is my IP? IP Snoop answers this question. This light-weight app for Windows Phone 7 shows your current IP-address. Supporting live tiles, IP Snoop allows you to keep up-to-date about your current IP-address.
